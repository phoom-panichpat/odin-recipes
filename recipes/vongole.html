Spaghetti Vongole

Description

My favorite dish

A quick and easy menu

    Ingredients

  • Calms
  • spaghetti
  • salts
  • olive oil
  • water
  • dry chili pepper
  • garlic

    Steps

  • put in the following ingredients in the pan:
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil
  • 60 ml of water
  • 120g. of calm meat
  • 2g. of salts
  • 1 chili pepper
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • heat the pan until water boil
  • after the water boil put in 60g. of spaghetti
  • cook for 4-6 until water evaporate and reduce
